Our Service

Protect Your Family with Best Flour

Cleaning

The wheat grains are cleaned to remove any foreign materials like stones sticks or other debris that may have been harvested with the wheat.

Tempering

Moisture is added to the wheat either by adding or removing water to reach a specific moisture content .

Separating

The grounds wheat is then swifted to separate the different components: bran, germ and endosperm. The endosperm is further refined to create white flour.
